# Setu Impact Report: Changing the Face of Indian Agriculture

## 1. The Crisis: The Invisible Majority

India has roughly **260 million** agricultural workers.
- **85%** are "Small and Marginal Farmers" (owning less than 2 hectares).
- **Literacy Rate** in rural India is ~73%, but **Digital Functional Literacy** is less than 10%.

### The Middleman Tax
Because these farmers cannot access digital markets/mandis directly, they sell to local intermediaries (Arhatiyas).
- **Commission**: Farmers lose **2-10%** of their crop value in commissions.
- **Price Opacity**: They have no way of knowing the real market price in the city 100km away.

---

## 2. The Setu Intervention

Setu serves as a "Digital Public Infrastructure" layer that democratizes access to the **Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC)**.

### Economic Impact
1.  **Price Discovery**: By connecting a farmer in a village directly to a buyer in a Metro city (e.g., BigBasket in Mumbai buying from a farmer in Nasik), we eliminate the information asymmetry.
2.  **Increased Margin**: Eliminating the middleman can increase the farmer's net income by **15-20%** instantly.
3.  **Market Expansion**: A farmer is no longer limited to his local Mandi. He has a national audience.

### Social Impact
1.  **Inclusion**: We bring the "Internet of Commerce" to those who cannot read/write, preventing a digital caste system.
2.  **Dignity**: Providing a professional, high-tech tool to a farmer validates their role as a business owner, not just a laborer.
3.  **Preservation of Language**: By supporting vernacular voice inputs, we allow technology to adapt to the user, not forcing the user to learn English.

---

## 3. Scalability & National Potential

Setu is built to be a standard-bearer for the "AI for Bharat" mission.

### Standardization (Beckn Protocol)
Because Setu outputs standardized **Beckn Protocol** JSON, it is interoperable with *any* ONDC Buyer App.
- It works with Paytm.
- It works with PhonePe.
- It works with Craftsvilla.
**One integration scales to the entire nation.**

### Infrastructure Efficiency
- **Cloud Agnostic**: Runs on Docker. Can be hosted on NIC (National Informatics Centre) servers, AWS, or GCP.
- **Low Cost**: The AI cost is pennies per transaction. The ROI is massive.

## 4. Case Study Simulation (The Nasik Onion)

*Based on current market data.*

**Scenario**:
- Farmer Ramesh has 500kg of Onions.
- **Local Mandi Price**: ₹15/kg (Dictated by local cartel).
- **Total Income**: ₹7,500.

**With Setu**:
- Ramesh speaks to Setu.
- Setu checks `data.gov.in` and sees prices in Mumbai are ₹25/kg.
- Setu broadcasts to ONDC.
- **Buyer (Reliance Retail)** offers ₹22/kg (including logistics cost).
- **Total Income**: ₹11,000.

**Net Gain**: **₹3,500 (+46% increase)** in a single transaction.
